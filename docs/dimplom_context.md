# Diploma context

## Topic
Система мониторинга температурных аномалий с использованием БПЛА и инфракрасных датчиков.

## What is being modeled
We model the infrared sensor signal formation process and generate synthetic thermal images.

## Important concepts
- Planck law
- Stefan–Boltzmann law
- power distribution over sensor surface
- microbolometer response
- readout chain
- noise sources
- non-uniformity correction
- defective pixels

## Desired outputs for diploma
- plots of signal vs temperature
- plots of SNR vs temperature / noise level
- synthetic image examples
- explanation of how noise and non-uniformity affect detectability
- experiment summaries suitable for thesis chapters
